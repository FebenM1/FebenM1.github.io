GUID FOR FIRST GENERATION STUDENTS APPLYING TO COLLEGE

As a first generation student myself I didn't have anyone at home who knew what the college process was like or what steps that I needed to take to properly get ready for it, so i had to do all the necessary research on my own. By making this website I hope to help others who are in the same boat at least have some understanding of what they need to do.
